🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM — LIGHT ANALYTICAL MODE

The dashboard must use a light, institutional, professional color palette.

Background

Main background: very light grey (#F5F7FA or similar)

Cards: pure white (#FFFFFF)

Borders: subtle grey (#E3E6EB)

No dark mode as default

Accent Colors

Neutral regime → muted blue

Fragile → soft amber

Risk-Off → soft red

Constructive → soft green

No aggressive neon or trading-style colors.

Typography must be clean, modern, readable (Inter, SF Pro, or similar).

The visual identity should resemble:

Institutional research dashboard

Risk analytics platform

Professional macro intelligence tool

Not crypto terminal.
Not Bloomberg clone.
Not trading app.
